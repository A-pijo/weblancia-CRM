## Weekly Routine
1. **Monday**: Submit 10 directories (30 min)
2. **Tuesday**: Respond to 1-2 HARO/PR queries (15 min)
3. **Wednesday**: Publish 1 social media post + engage Reddit (20 min)
4. **Thursday**: Submit 10 more directories (30 min)
5. **Friday**: Track submissions, update spreadsheet (15 min)

## Tools to Use
- **HARO Monitoring**: Featured.com (free)
- **Backlink Tracking**: Google Search Console (free) + Ahrefs free checker
- **Directory Tracking**: Spreadsheet with status columns
- **Automation**: SaaSLaunch ($29/yr) or SubmitSaaS ($3/dir)
- **Citation Tracking**: BrightLocal or Whitespark

## Risk Mitigation
- **Avoid**: Link farms, auto-submit services that blast identical content
- **Diversity**: Ensure link profile has mix of dofollow/nofollow, varied anchor text
- **Pacing**: Spread submissions over weeks, not hours
- **Quality**: Reject directories with spam score >30% or DA <10 (unless highly relevant)
- **Updates**: Keep all profile information consistent and up-to-date

## Success Metrics to Track
- Number of live backlinks (weekly)
- Domain Rating change (monthly)
- Referring domains count (monthly)
- Organic traffic trend (weekly)
- Keyword rankings for "agence digitale Maroc" (weekly)
- New leads from directory listings (monthly)
